INDICATIONS AND USAGE
Image-Conflict is indicated for cognitive and perceptual distortion arising from manipulation or decontextualization of images. It enhances the impact of such representations on collective understanding through alterations of media narratives and the influence of social platforms spreading polarizing visual content. Not recommended for individuals exposed to unverified image flows or sudden shifts in public opinion.

WARNINGS AND PRECAUTIONS
Excessive or uncontrolled use may result in: Misperception of public figures, Dehumanization of subjects, Misalignment between perceived and objective reality. Image-Conflict may contribute to the creation of parallel truths mediated by the media, influencing moral judgments and political decisions on a large scale. Use with caution in environments with high exposure to emotionally charged visual content.

ADVERSE REACTIONS

  • Emotional polarization
  • Opinion conflicts
  • Public disputes
  • Context collapse
  • Parasocial tendencies
